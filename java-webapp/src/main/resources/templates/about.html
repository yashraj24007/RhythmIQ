About RhythmIQ

Advanced AI-powered ECG analysis for better cardiac care

Our Mission

RhythmIQ is an AI-powered ECG monitoring system designed to analyze heart signals in real time. Our mission is to democratize access to advanced cardiac analysis by providing healthcare professionals with powerful, accurate, and easy-to-use diagnostic tools.

We process electrocardiogram (ECG) data, detect irregularities, and classify them into common arrhythmia types while predicting severity levels to help healthcare providers prioritize urgent cases and optimize patient outcomes.

ECG Classifications Supported

N Normal Beats

Sinus rhythm, bundle branch blocks, and other normal cardiac patterns.

S Supraventricular Beats

Atrial premature beats and supraventricular ectopic rhythms.

V Ventricular Beats

Premature Ventricular Contractions (PVCs) and ventricular ectopics.

F Fusion Beats

Combination of ventricular and normal beats occurring simultaneously.

Q Unknown/Paced Beats

Paced beats and unclassifiable cardiac patterns.

M Myocardial Infarction

Heart attack indicators and ischemic changes.

Severity Assessment

Mild

Low risk condition requiring routine monitoring and follow-up care.

Moderate

Medium risk condition requiring closer monitoring and medical attention.

Severe

High risk condition requiring immediate medical intervention and care.
