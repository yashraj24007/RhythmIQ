At RhythmIQ, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, name, and login credentials
  • Usage Data: Analysis history, timestamps, and interaction data
  • ECG Images: Temporarily processed and automatically deleted

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our ECG analysis services
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • Improve our AI models (with anonymized data only)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, secure data transmission, regular security audits, and access controls.

Data Retention

ECG Images: Automatically deleted after analysis completion. Account Data: Retained until account deletion requested. Analysis History: Can be deleted by users at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete your personal data, export your data, and opt-out of communications.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management, preference cookies for theme settings, and analytics cookies to understand usage patterns.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting and analytics. These services are carefully vetted and bound by privacy agreements.
